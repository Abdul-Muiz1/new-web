Fufu Recipes

Home page

Fufu is a local Ghanaian dish made from yam or cassava and sometimes with plantain. The yam is boiled and pounded either with a local mortar and pistol or a machine

Ingredients

  • Yam
  • cassava
  • garden eggs
  • pepper
  • onions
  • spicies
  • meat

Steps

  1. peal the yam and cassava
  2. Boil them until they become very soft
  3. now let the yam and cassava cool off a bit before
  4. put it in your machine or local mortar and pistol and pound it
  5. afterwards,now make it look smooth with your hands
  6. then serve and eat with your soup
