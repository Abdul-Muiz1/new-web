Banku Recipes

Home page

Banku is a local Ghanaian dish made from Corn/maize which are soaked in water for a period of time to ferment before being grind to Mole which is the flour for the flour for the banku.

Ingredients

  • Maize/corn
  • garden eggs
  • pepper
  • onions
  • spicies
  • meat

Steps

  1. Soak the maize for 2 days
  2. then take it to the grinding machine and grind it
  3. let it stay fir about some hours to days without proper ventilation
  4. now to cook it, add some of the flour to water and heat it whist stirring
  5. wait till it becomes very hard and then you stir well to mix properly
  6. then serve and eat with your soup
